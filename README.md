## Business Problem
Retail management required a clear view of sales performance across outlet size, location, and product categories to support inventory planning and expansion decisions.

## Dataset Overview
The dataset contains sales data across multiple outlet types, locations, item categories, and customer ratings.

## Tools Used
- Excel (data preprocessing)

## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Average Sales per Item
- Number of Items Sold
- Average Customer Ratings

## Key Insights
- Medium-sized outlets generated the highest sales, indicating optimal operational efficiency.
- Tier-1 locations contributed the maximum revenue, while Tier-3 showed growth potential.
- Fruits & Vegetables and Snack Foods were the top-performing categories.
- Regular fat products slightly outperformed low-fat products, indicating taste-driven demand.

## Business Recommendations
- Focus expansion strategies on medium-sized outlets in Tier-1 cities.
- Improve marketing and assortment planning for low-performing categories.
- Use demand trends to balance health-focused vs regular product offerings.
